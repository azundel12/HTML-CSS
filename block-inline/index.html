Basic document flow

I am a basic block level element. My adjacent block level element sit on new lines below me.

By default we span 100% of the width of our parent element, and are as tall as our child content. Our total width and height is our content + padding + border width/height

We are separated by our margins. Because of margin collapsing, we are separated by the size of one of our margins, not both.
